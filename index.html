How to Play

  • Use Arrow Left / Right or A / D to move your spaceship.
  • Press Space or Arrow Up to shoot lasers.
  • Destroy enemy ships to score points.
  • Collect power-ups dropped by enemies: extra lives or double guns.
  • Don't let enemies hit you or reach the bottom of the screen.
  • Survive as long as you can and climb levels to increase difficulty.

Good luck, pilot!
